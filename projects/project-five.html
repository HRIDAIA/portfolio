HCW Rendezvous Guidance Toolkit

This case study will document a lightweight Hill-Clohessy-Wiltshire (HCW) rendezvous sandbox that I’m building to prototype cooperative guidance logic ahead of higher-fidelity simulation. It layers analytical propagation, Monte Carlo campaigns, and ROS 2 visualization nodes to quickly iterate on controller ideas.

I’m organizing write-ups that cover the core trajectory generators, how I incorporate sensor errors, and how results feed into the multi-agent simulator. Reach out if you’d like to see interim plots or discuss extending the framework.
